## Beliefs
1. There is power in a union.
2. The Vaylen Figure of Note is the best smuggler in the galaxy.
3. This planet won't run without Trent Spires.

## Instincts

3. Always have an escape plan arranged.

### Trait Descriptions

**Mark of Privilege** (Dt, req'd) — 1D affiliation with nobility. +1 Ob to Inconspicuous or Falsehood when masquerading as lower station.

**Vig** (Dt, req'd) — Loans carry interest equal to the obstacle +1-3. Unpaid debts earn the debtor 1D infamous reputation among the family; the family will collect by any means. Repayment cancels the reputation and reprisals.

**Savvy** (C-O) — Call-on for Circles when implicitly threatening the use of force — physical, social, financial or otherwise.

**Determined** (C-O) — Reroll any one failed skill/stat/attribute test associated with one of his Beliefs. Once per phase.
